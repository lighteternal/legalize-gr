In many cases they prepared the ground for a more intense co-operation based on institutional partnerships and national or European programs. At the end of 2004 the last 22 bilateral projects will be finished. Both sides underlined the importance of the project-ori?ented co-operation scheme of IKY and DAAD (IKYDA), which will provide an appropriate umbrella for joint projec?ts. Here, at the moment 19 projects are supported for a funding period of 01.01.2003 to 31.12.2005 . b.
