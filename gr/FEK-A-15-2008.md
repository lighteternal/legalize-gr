Κύρωση του Μνημονίου Συνεργασίας μεταξύ της Κυβέρνησης της Ελληνικής Δημοκρατίας και της Κυβέρνησης της Δημοκρατίας του Αζερμπαϊτζάν στον τομέα του Φυσικού Αερίου και του Πετρελαίου.

**ΤΗΣ ΕΛΛΗΝΙΚΗΣ ΔΗΜΟΚΡΑΤΙΑΣ**

The Parties shall assess, evaluate the possibility and study the related terms of selling and transiting through pipelines natural gas originating from the Republic of Azerbaijan, to Greece destined for the domestic market and also for transit through the Poseidon pipeline to Italy and other European countries. Article 3 The Parties will exchange relevant information, which may be of interest to each other or may assist them in making their decisions regarding the above mentioned objectives. Article 4 The Parties will establish a Joint Sub-Committee in the framework of the Joint Intergovernmental Committee on Economic, Industrial and Technological Cooperation between the Hellenic Republic and the Republic of Azerbaijan in order to examine the above mentioned objectives in articles 1 and 2 and choose which ever option that achieves the interests of both Parties. The Joint Sub-Committee shall meet in Athens or in Baku, to be decided through diplomatic channels. The first meeting of the Joint Sub-Committee shall be held within two (2) months from the date of the entry into force of the Memorandum. Article 5 The Memorandum does not constitute any selling, purchasing nor transfer obligations to either Party. Article 6 The present Memorandum shall enter into force on the date of the last written notification by which the Parties inform each other through diplomatic channels of the completion of the necessary legal procedures. either Party notifies in writing the other, through diplomatic channels, at least six (6) months prior to the expiration of the initial or subsequent period, of its intention to terminate the present Memorandum.
