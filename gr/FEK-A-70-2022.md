# Προεδρικά Διατάγματα — ΦΕΚ A' 70/2022

ΠΕΡΙΕΧΟΜΕΝΑ ΠΡΟΕΔΡΙΚΑ ΔΙΑΤΑΓΜΑΤΑ 1 27. Σύσταση Άμισθης Προξενικής Αρχής της Ελλάδας στην πόλη Ανδόρα λα Βέλια της Ανδόρας ΑΠΟΦΑΣΕΙΣ 2 Έγκριση του Πρωτοκόλλου της Τέταρτης Συνόδου της Μικτής Ελληνο-Τουρκικής Επιτροπής Τουρισμού. ΠΡΟΕΔΡΙΚΑ ΔΙΑΤΑΓΜΑΤΑ

# ΠΡΟΕΔΡΙΚΟ ΔΙΑΤΑΓΜΑ ΥΠ’ ΑΡΙΘΜ. 27 (1)

Σύσταση Άμισθης Προξενικής Αρχής της Ελλάδας στην πόλη Ανδόρα λα Βέλια της Ανδόρας.

**Η ΠΡΟΕΔΡΟΣ**

**ΤΗΣ ΕΛΛΗΝΙΚΗΣ ΔΗΜΟΚΡΑΤΙΑΣ**

Έχοντας υπόψη:

1. Την παράγραφο 3 του άρθρου 308 του ν.4781/2021 «Οργάνωση και λειτουργία Υπουργείου Εξωτερικών, Συμβούλιο Απόδημου Ελληνισμού, ρύθμιση θεμάτων διεθνούς αναπτυξιακής συνεργασίας και ανθρωπιστικής βοήθειας και άλλες διατάξεις» (Α΄31).

2. Τη γνώμη της Πρεσβείας της Ελλάδας στη Μαδρίτη, όπως διατυπώθηκε στο υπό στοιχεία Α.Π. 1141/17.09.2021 έγγραφό της.

3. Το γεγονός ότι από την εφαρμογή των διατάξεων του παρόντος διατάγματος δεν προκαλείται δαπάνη σε βάρος του κρατικού προϋπολογισμού.

4. Την υπ΄αρ. 11/2022 γνωμοδότηση του Συμβουλίου της Επικρατείας, με πρόταση του Υπουργού Εξωτερικών, αποφασίζουμε:

##### Άρθρο 1

Σύσταση Άμισθου Προξενείου της Ελλάδας στην πόλη Ανδόρα λα Βέλια του Πριγκιπάτου της Ανδόρας Συστήνεται Άμισθο Προξενείο της Ελλάδας με έδρα την πόλη Ανδόρα λα Βέλια (Andorra la Vella) του Πριγκιπάτου της Ανδόρας και περιφέρεια αρμοδιότητας όλη την επικράτεια του Πριγκιπάτου.

##### Άρθρο 2

Έναρξη ισχύος Η ισχύς του παρόντος διατάγματος αρχίζει από τη δημοσίευσή του στην Εφημερίδα της Κυβερνήσεως. Στον Υπουργό Εξωτερικών αναθέτουμε τη δημοσίευση και εκτέλεση του παρόντος διατάγματος. Αθήνα,17 Μαρτίου 2022 Η Πρόεδρος της Δημοκρατίας ΚΑΤΕΡΙΝΑ ΣΑΚΕΛΛΑΡΟΠΟΥΛΟΥ Ο Υπουργός Εξωτερικών ΝΙΚΟΛΑΟΣ - ΓΕΩΡΓΙΟΣ ΔΕΝΔΙΑΣ Ι ΑΠΟΦΑΣΕΙΣ Αριθμ. 0544/Μ.7489/ΑΣ 3499 (2) Έγκριση του Πρωτοκόλλου της Τέταρτης Συνόδου της Μικτής Ελληνο-Τουρκικής Επιτροπής Τουρισμού. 1. Την Συμφωνία μεταξύ της Ελληνικής Δημοκρατίας και της Δημοκρατίας της Τουρκίας για συνεργασία στον Τομέα του Τουρισμού, που υπογράφηκε στην Άγκυρα, στις 20 Ιανουαρίου 2000 και κυρώθηκε με τον ν. 2897/2001 (Α΄69).

2. Το άρθρο δεύτερο του ανωτέρω κυρωτικού νόμου, σύμφωνα με το οποίο τα Πρωτόκολλα-Πρακτικά τα οποία καταρτίζονται από τη Μικτή Επιτροπή του άρθρου 11 της Συμφωνίας εγκρίνονται με Κοινή Πράξη των κατά περίπτωση αρμόδιων Υπουργών.

5. Το π.δ. 68/2021 «Διορισμός Υπουργών, Αναπληρώτριας Υπουργού και Υφυπουργών» (Α΄155).

The Greek Delegation was headed by Mrs Vasiliki Loizou, Secretary General for Tourism Policy and Development, and the Turkish Delegation was headed by Mr. Nadir Alpaslan, Deputy Minister of Ministry of Culture and Tourism of Turkey. The Session was held in a friendly atmosphere, the Heads of the two delegations exchanged brief information concerning the development of bilateral tourism cooperation and the needed actions and measures for the recovery of the sector in both countries from the effect of the Covid - 19 pandemic. The members of the two Delegations are mentioned in Annex I of this Protocol. The two Delegations will, hereinafter, be referred to as "the Participants". The agenda of the Session was adopted and is attached as Annex II of this Protocol.

1. Review of bilateral relations in the field of tourism cooperation since the last meeting The Participants expressed their satisfaction about the progress achieved in their bilateral cooperation in the field of tourism, mentioning the Third Session of the Greek-Turkish Joint Tourism Committee Meeting and the 8th Greek-Turkish Forum on Tourism held in Athens, on 12th of October 2011, at Ministerial level, with the participation of representatives from the private sector.

2. Discussion of the current Covid - 19 situations in both countries and relevant safety travel prerequisites The Participants emphasized the importance of cooperation as neighboring countries, especially in the field of tourism, which was adversely affected during the pandemic period. The Participants exchanged information concerning the current Covid - 19 situations in both countries and relevant safety travel prerequisites of their respective countries and agreed to support the mutual tourism flow and to provide to all interested tourism stakeholders the necessary information about the safety travel prerequisites in Greece and Turkey.

3. Sharing the experiences in certifying hotels and accommodation places by the Covid - 19 regulations The Greek side presented the "Health First" certification for hotels and other accommodation establishments. The Turkish side presented its "Safe Tourism" certification program for hotels and other accommodation places. The Participants discussed about the exchange of information and successful practices concerning the certification of the hotels and other accommodation facilities as well as any other administrative regulations related to the Covid - 19 pandemic.

4. Encouraging the bilateral relations of NGOs in tourism sector The Participants agreed on encouraging the establishment of direct contact between the relevant institutions of Greece and Turkey in the field of tourism business (tour operators, travel agents, tourism associations and travel journalists), recommended the organization of study tours for journalists and tour operators and agreed to exchange information in the field of tourism marketing. The Participants agreed to encourage their relevant organizations to explore possibilities to participate in tourism activities like specialized tourism fairs, tourism exhibitions, trade fairs, festivals, seminars, congresses and workshops, organized in their countries. Turkish side invited Greek tourism professionals to participate in "Travel Turkey Izmir" tourism fair which will be held in Izmir 2-4 December 2021 and EMITT tourism fair which will be held in Istanbul, on 9-12 February 2022.

5. Cooperation in the framework of international tourism organizations The Participants consider that the development of the cooperation between the competent Ministries of their countries in the framework of regional and international

6. Discussion about the digital future of the tourism industry The Participants endeavor to cooperate to facilitate tourism industry stakeholders in better implementing the digitalization innovations and processes in the sector.

7. Promotion and elaboration of joint tourist packages for emerging tourist markets Taking into consideration that tourism is an effective tool for fostering economic development, both Participants will further encourage the activities of their tourism private sectors, concerning the promotion and elaboration of common products in the field of historical, natural and cultural heritage as a combined tourist package for long-haul destinations, especially major overseas markets like China, USA, Japan and India.

8. Investment opportunities in the field of tourism - incentives The Participants will explore the opportunities of cooperation in the field of tourism investment and for this purpose they will inform each other about existing investment opportunities as well as the investment incentives applied in their countries in the field of tourism.

9. Improving the existing air and ferry lines connections between the two countries With a view to intensifying the tourist flows between their respective countries, the Participants will explore the possibilities of cooperation on improving the existing air and ferry lines connections and infrastructure between the two countries.

10. Cooperation in the field of maritime and cruise tourism The Participants agreed to further cooperating and sharing experience in the field of maritime and cruise tourism.

11. Place and date of the 5th meeting of the Greek-Turkish Joint Tourism Committee Both Participants agreed to arrange the next meeting of the Joint Committee in Greece. The time and place will be decided later by mutual agreement of the Participants. The Greek Side expressed its appreciation for the warm hospitality of the Turkish Side as well as for the results achieved during the 4th meeting of the Joint Tourism Committee. obligations arising from its membership to the European Union.
